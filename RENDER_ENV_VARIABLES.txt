HOW TO ADD TO RENDER:
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com/
2. Select your service (vaanikaam backend)
3. Click "Environment" on the left sidebar
4. Click "Add Environment Variable"
5. For each variable above:
   - Copy the name (e.g., FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID)
   - Paste it in the "Key" field
   - Copy the value (e.g., vaanikaam-firebase)
   - Paste it in the "Value" field
   - Click "Save"

6. After adding all 3 variables, click "Deploy" button to restart your service
